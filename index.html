✦ FEATURED PROJECT
NBA Player Compensation & Performance BI Analysis
Business Intelligence Developer | Data Science

A full Business Intelligence lifecycle project analyzing whether NBA salaries align with player performance efficiency. Follows the CRISP-DM framework from raw multi-source data ingestion through statistical modeling to prescriptive roster optimization and delivering real, actionable front-office recommendations.

  • Multi-source Python pipeline (nba_api, Pandas, fuzzywuzzy) merging 2,500+ player-season records from Basketball-Reference and Spotrac with a 99.4% name-match accuracy rate via fuzzy string matching
  • Multiple Linear Regression (Adj. R²=.74) identified True Shooting % as the dominant salary predictor: a 1-unit gain corresponds to a 4.2% salary increase, outperforming raw PPG
  • K-Means Clustering (k=4, Silhouette=0.61) revealed a persistent $18–20M salary arbitrage gap between "Efficient Specialist" defenders and overpaid legacy contracts
  • Linear Programming roster optimizer maximized Win Shares under the $140.6M NBA salary cap, projecting 12% more wins than the average playoff team at $15M less in cap spend
